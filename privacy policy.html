Privacy Policy

We take customer privacy very seriously. Our privacy policy is designed to protect the personal information of our customers. We will never sell or share your personal information without your consent. We also maintain the right to change our privacy policy at any time, so please check it regularly for updates. This policy helps us target our marketing efforts more effectively and ensure that you have confidence in the way we use your personal information.

Flightocart collects the following information: -Your name
-Your email address
-The date and time of your ticket purchase
-The airline(s) and flight number(s) you have purchased tickets for
Flightocartdoes not collect any other personal information.


How will Flightocart use this information?

Your flight tickets will use this information to provide you with the best possible service. We will use your name, email address, and flight number to contact you about your ticket purchase and other Flightocart services. We may also use this information to contact you about our products and services that may be of interest to you. You can unsubscribe from these communications at any time by clicking on the "unsubscribe" link in the email we send or by contacting us.


How can I change or delete my information?

If you would like to change or delete your information, please follow these steps: 1. Log in to your account. 2. Click on "Account Settings." 3. Under "Privacy & Security," click on "Change Your Privacy Settings." 4. On the next screen, under "Personal Information," select which items you would like to change or delete. 5. Click on the blue "Submit" button at the bottom of the page to make your changes or deletions.


What if I have questions about Flightocart privacy policy?

If you have any questions about the privacy policy of Flightocart, please visit our website or contact us. We would be happy to answer any questions that you may have.
